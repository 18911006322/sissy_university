Punishments

If you fail to attend a class by the end of the day, do one of the daily tasks or fail an exam you must press the corresponding button to roll a random punishment. Clubs

For every club activity done you get one perk use corresponding to that activity. This means that doing Club Activity 1 grants you one use of Perk 1. You can stack as many perk uses as activities you have done. Perk uses are stored and can be used later. Activities can be done on any day of the week. Choose a club depending on your major and interests. Some clubs become very useful later on. Leaving a club will reset all of your perks and club activities.

Graduating

Once you have completed all your major’s prerequisite classes and those classes’ prerequisite classes, completed the required number of core/base/advanced/master classes, completed all your current classes and done all of your pending punishments, you may attempt to graduate.
